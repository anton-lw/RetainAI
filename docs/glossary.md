# Glossary

## Beneficiary

An individual or household receiving program services, support, or monitoring.

## Retention

Continued participation or continued successful engagement with the program, as operationally defined by the deploying organization.

## Disengagement

Loss of recorded interaction within a relevant period. In many deployments this is a better operational target than an abstract “true dropout.”

## Dropout label

The configured rule used to define the prediction target, such as no recorded interaction within a specified window.

## Top-K

The highest-ranked cases the program can realistically act on, based on staffing and capacity.

## Shadow mode

A validation mode where the live queue is captured and later compared against observed outcomes without immediately treating it as the sole operational basis for action.

## Fairness review

Assessment of whether the model’s behavior varies materially across relevant groups.

## Drift

Change in data or model behavior over time that can reduce reliability.

## Soft indicators

Structured qualitative signals captured by field staff, such as household stability or economic stress.
